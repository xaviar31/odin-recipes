Mrs.Sigg's Snickerdoodles

Description:

This is a simple recipe for snickerdoodles, which are cinnamon sugar cookies. These cookies will have crispy edges with chewy centers. These cookies will be an ultimate crowd pleaser, or just simple home enjoyed cookies. Trust me, you will love this recipe.

This recipe will take approximately 20 minutes of preparation and 10 minutes of cook time, with an additional 30mins to create, for a total of 1hr. This recipe will create about 48 servings yielding 4 dozen cookies. Each serving will be about 92 calories, have 1g of protein, 12.4g of carbs, 4.3g fat, 12.8mg cholesterol and 12.8mg sodium.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter, soft
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups sugar, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until smooth. Add flour, cream of tartar, soda, and salt; beat just until combined. Shape dough by rounded spoonfuls into balls.
  3. Place 2 tablespoons sugar in a small bowl; stir in cinnamon. Roll balls of dough in mixture. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until set but not too hard. Remove immediately from baking sheets.
