The Best Brownies Recipe

Description:

This is a quick and easy brownie recipe that will leave you with the best brownies. These brownies will be just right and come together in the oven in less than 5 minutes! If you love smooth, sweet, and perfect brownies, than this recipe is just for you. These aren't called "The best brownies" for no reason, for these brownies are moist, rich, and will melt in your mouth.

This recipe will take approximately 15 minutes of preparation and 30 minutes of cook time, leaving it to a total of 45 minutes to create! This recipe will create about 16 servings being 16 brownies. Each serving will be about 183 calories, have 1.8g of protein, 25.7g of carbs, 9g fat, 44.2mg cholesterol and 109.7mg sodium.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Frosting:

  • 3 tbsp butter, softened
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sugar

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease and flour an 8in pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter(non softened). Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Beat in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in oven for 25-30mins.
  4. Remove from oven and make the frosting. Combine softened butter, cocoa, honey, vanilla, and sugar. Stir until smooth. (only use ingredients from frosting section of ingredients)
  5. Frost the brownies while they are still warm.
